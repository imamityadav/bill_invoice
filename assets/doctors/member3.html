Premature ejaculation (Early Discharge)
Premature ejaculation is an uncontrolled ejaculation wherein the man expels his semen prior or immediately after vaginal penetration with minimal stimulation. This condition is also called fast ejaculation, quick ejaculation, sheegra patan (seegra patan), shighrapatan, rapid ejaculation, rapid climax etc. Premature Ejaculation is characterized by early ejaculation of the semen before the man experience complete satisfaction. A man with premature ejaculation finds himself guilty for not satisfying his sexual partner. Early ejaculation leads to less or truncated sexual pleasure and may bring dissatisfaction among the partners. The causes of premature ejaculation are not clearly known. When the desire for sex is below average it is termed as low sex desire or low sex drive. According to a study, approximately 1 in 5 men suffer from low sex drive at some point of their life. The loss of libido leads to dissatisfaction in opposite partner. Sometimes even the men who are suffering from low libido get frustrated because of this. Age is one of the factors for low sex desire, but not always. Several health disorders like diabetes, high blood pressure, anxiety, stress, depression, obesity, drugs, medications and chronic diseases can cause low sex desire in men. Decrease in testosterone hormone level also causes low sex libido. Low sex drive occurs both in men and women.
